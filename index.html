Finding apartments on the Upper East Side of Manhattan

We have lived in the Upper East Side /Yorkville/Spanish Harlem zip codes for eight years now. During this time we have seen the neighbourhood evolve and change.We have moved around in the area quite a bit and have had to deal with a lot of Agents and Management Companies. As a rule of thumb our rent goes up and we move from one zip code to the next. Hence this attempt at sharing what we know. Cross reference this list against yelp reviews and other websites that collate user generated reviews.

The following list is a summation of our experiences and those of our friends.
